## How to fill the metadata

- `priority`: `primary`, `secondary`, `maintenance`, or `parked`
- `status`: `active`, `waiting`, `paused`, or `done`
- `flexibility`: `fixed`, `movable`, or `deferrable`
- `required_blocks_7d` / `required_blocks_14d`: how many `90`-minute focus blocks this goal realistically needs in the next `7` and `14` days
- `minimum_viable_blocks_7d`: the smallest next-7-day version that still counts as real progress
