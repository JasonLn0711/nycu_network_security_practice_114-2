# HW01 Expected Output

A complete study packet should include:

- A short explanation of the target program or practice binary.
- The commands used to inspect files, permissions, and execution behavior.
- Notes on important strings, functions, branches, and input checks.
- Ghidra evidence: imported binary, auto-analysis status, relevant functions, xrefs, and decompiler observations.
- A final answer or hypothesis, clearly separated from raw evidence.

## Evidence Checklist

- Commands are copyable.
- Screenshots or exported notes are named clearly.
- Observations distinguish facts from guesses.
- Any unsafe or unknown binary is handled in a controlled local environment.
- Reusable concepts are linked back to `lectures/` or `labs/` rather than duplicated.
